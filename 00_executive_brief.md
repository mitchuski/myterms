## Who We Are

agentprivacy is a dual-agent protocol architecture. We're building the Swordsman browser agent, which implements IEEE 7012-2025 as the **agreement layer** — the bilateral protocol on which enforcement is built, not the enforcement itself. Not a wallet. Not an identity system. The Swordsman implements the agreement; the Mage operates inside it; together they produce the agent separation that the standard alone does not require. The protocol layer between protection and delegation is a communication substrate that every community can extend with their own secret language while maintaining interoperability. Privacy Value Model V5.4 scores the three axes — agent separation (Σ), data (Δ), and inference (Γ) — multiplicatively, so architectural shortcuts are visible rather than averaged away.
